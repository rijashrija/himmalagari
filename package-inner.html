Trip Itinerary

The representatives of the Best Adventures Travel shall welcome you to the bustling city of Kathmandu at Tribhuvan International Airport (TIA) and transfer you to the hotel. Rest of the day can be spent exploring Thamel. In the evening, the leader will brief the travel schedules, introduce the guides and members, followed by a welcome dinner with authentic Nepali cuisine.

Early morning flight to Lukla followed by a short and scenic trek to Phakding through Sherpa villages, prayer wheels, and suspension bridges.

Trek along the Dudh Koshi River, cross suspension bridges, and make the final ascent to the vibrant Sherpa town of Namche Bazaar.

Acclimatization day with short hikes around Namche, including visits to Everest View Hotel or Khumjung village.
